Data

  1. We are going to use a dataset from an HIV study. Read the dataset hivinfection weight into R We will be using the variables:
  1. weight_kg - child birth weight
  2. cd4 maternal CD4 count at delivery
  3. momhb Maternal Haemoglobin levels at delivery
  4. ga_weeks gestational age at delivery/birth
  5. hiv_status Childs HIV status at birth
  6. sex - the sex of the child
  1. The primary research question is Is child birth weight associated with child HIV status?
  • Adjust for any confounders and fit a multi-variable model
  1. The primary research question is Is child birth weight associated with maternal CD4 count at birth?
  • Adjust for any confounders and fit a multi-variable model
